Welcome to Shapy.js

Use Shapy.js for all your geometric dom manipulation, such as specifying shape alignments for elements, rotating around in shapes, animating elements, moving elements and many more!

Let's say we want to display a diagram of the Moon's orbit around the Earth. We can do that by calling positionElementCircle on our moon element class. If for some reason, we wanted the moon's to have a square orbit, we could do that easily by calling positionElementSquare on our moon class. If you want to watch the moon's actually orbit, we can simulate this by using the rotateElements function. Click on one of them moons in each orbit, to watch them rotate around the Earth!
